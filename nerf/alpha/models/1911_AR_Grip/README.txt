# Summary

-=VERSION 2=-
Modified the grip to be a slick sided skeletal grip instead of having cut outs for specific grip plates.  This is fairly comfortable even with out a grip plate installed.  This version should accept any standard 1911 Grip plate.  Grips with a back-strap may not work due to the ridge stippling built into the grip.

-=VERSION 1=-
This is a mod of a MOE Magpul inspired grip (By FP) to accept 1911 grip plates on the sides.  Will fit many AR style airsoft, Camera mods etc.  

For the grip plates just print the grip plate, mirror and print again.
